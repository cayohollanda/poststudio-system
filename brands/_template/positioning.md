# Positioning — `[brand-slug]`

> The one-liner positioning statement, the differentiation matrix, and the category framing. Distinct from `offer.md`'s "what we sell" — this file is *how the brand stands relative to alternatives*.

---

## Positioning statement

> One sentence the brand lives by.

**[BRAND]** is the **[CATEGORY]** for **[AUDIENCE]** who want **[OUTCOME]** without **[PAIN]**.

Filled-in example structures:

- "[BRAND] is the [TOOL/PLATFORM/SERVICE] for [SPECIFIC_ROLE] who [SPECIFIC_SITUATION] and want [SPECIFIC_OUTCOME] without [SPECIFIC_PAIN]."
- "[BRAND] turns [INPUT] into [OUTPUT] for [AUDIENCE], replacing [ALTERNATIVE]."
- "[BRAND] is the [CATEGORY] you actually use, not just install — for [AUDIENCE]."
