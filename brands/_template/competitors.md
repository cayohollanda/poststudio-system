## Off-limits framings

Per `constraints.md`:

- We do not name [Competitor X] in posts unless explicit permission.
- We do not run side-by-side comparisons with named competitors.
- We do not disparage by name.
- We do compete on positioning, not on calling out names.

When discussing the category, use "the most popular tool in [category]," "calendar-AI tools," "the spreadsheet you've been using."

---

## Why this file exists

The audience always has alternatives. Pretending they don't makes the brand look naive. This file:

- Trains every future post on honest comparison.
- Surfaces real wins (vs imagined wins).
- Calibrates expectations for the audience.
- Protects the brand from rigging comparisons (which audience smells immediately).
