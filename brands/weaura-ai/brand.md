## Elevator pitch

Most "AI productivity" tools for ops are either generic chat (no context) or another dashboard (more pivoting). WeAura is neither. It's an enterprise AI platform that grounds every answer in *your* runbooks, *your* observability stack, and *your* deploy timeline — and cites the source on every response. Read-only operations execute immediately. Anything that writes requires human approval. Self-hosted or SaaS. Deploy in minutes, not weeks.

## Why we exist

SRE / DevOps / Platform teams lose hours per week to context-switching across 5+ dashboards, runbook archaeology, and tribal knowledge that lives in Slack threads. Generic AI doesn't know any of it. WeAura exists because the answer to "why is `payment-api` restarting?" should appear in 1.4 seconds, with the source cited — not after 20 minutes of pivoting between Grafana, Loki, GitHub, and PagerDuty.

## What we will not become

- We will not be a generic chat tool. The grounding is the product.
- We will not require data to leave the customer's perimeter (self-hosted is first-class).
- We will not auto-execute write operations without human approval.
- We will not return answers without citing sources.

## How to pronounce / spell

- **WeAura** — pronounced "wee-AW-rah" (English) / "ui-aura" (Portuguese). Always written with a capital "W" and "A" (camel-case wordmark).
- **AI** — uppercase, separated by a space. Full form: "WeAura AI."
