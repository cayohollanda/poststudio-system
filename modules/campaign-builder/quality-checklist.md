# Quality Checklist — Campaign Builder

> Campaign-specific checks. Run before returning.

---

## Critical (any fail → don't ship)

- [ ] Single named primary CTA.
- [ ] Each post has a distinct role in the 5-act arc.
- [ ] No two posts argue the same point with different words.
- [ ] Proof posts cite real proof (or marked `[PROOF_NEEDED]`).
- [ ] Pacing alternates argument and proof — no 5-post stretches of the same beat.
- [ ] Final post drives the CTA; the post before pre-empts the top objection.
- [ ] Voice rules respected across the campaign.
- [ ] No invented numbers, customers, awards anywhere in the campaign.
- [ ] Each post can stand alone (audience can swipe even without seeing the prior post).
- [ ] Campaign assets (stories, screenshots, numbers) listed up-front.

## Quality (each fail → flag)

- [ ] Cadence respects brand capacity (e.g. not 7/week if the team is one person).
- [ ] Founder voice present at the right cadence.
- [ ] Proof drop in week ≥ 4 of a 30-day campaign (not premature).
- [ ] CTA progression: weak in early posts, explicit in late posts.
- [ ] Cross-platform adaptation noted per post (when multi-platform).
- [ ] Risks surfaced (timing, audience fatigue, missing inputs).
- [ ] No banned topics from `constraints.md` appear in the sequence.
- [ ] Distribution plan (first comments, repurposing) included.

---

## Anti-patterns

- **The slow build to nothing.** 13 posts of "we have something coming" → audience checks out by post 3.
- **The same hook, six times.** Vary the angle every post.
- **CTA in every post.** The audience tunes out. Save explicit CTA for acts 4-5.
- **Misaligned platforms.** Same post duplicated on LinkedIn and TikTok unchanged.
- **No proof in the middle.** Setup → reframe → ask, no evidence in between, reads as marketing.
- **Founder absence.** A 30-day authority campaign with no founder voice.
- **The "we" parade.** Every post is "we built / we shipped / we believe." Vary perspective.

---

## Approval

A campaign passes when all critical pass and ≥ 6 of 8 quality items pass. Otherwise return with explicit gaps.

If a campaign would underperform on the chosen platform, say so explicitly — even at the cost of the brief.
