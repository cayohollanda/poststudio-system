# Launch Campaign Template (14 days)

> The standard 14-day product / feature / brand launch arc. Adapt for 7-day or 30-day windows.

---

## Arc

```
Days 1-3   — Setup (3 posts): name the problem, the audience, the shift.
Days 4-6   — Pressure (3 posts): cost of the default, what's broken.
Days 7-9   — Reframe (3 posts): the new way, the mechanism.
Days 10-12 — Proof (3 posts): receipts, demos, case.
Days 13-14 — Ask (2 posts): launch + objection handling.
```

---

## Per-post structure

```markdown
### Day [N] — [post role in arc]

- **Format:** [carousel / reel / essay / single image]
- **Angle:** [from system/content-system.md]
- **Hook:** [one line, ≤14 words]
- **Body summary:** [2-3 sentences]
- **CTA:** [the post's CTA, may differ from campaign primary]
- **Platform priority:** [linkedin / instagram / tiktok / x / multi]
- **Proof needed:** [list, or "none"]
- **Notes:** [non-obvious production note]
```

## Worked example (Lumen, 14-day launch of v3.4 Focus Mode)

### Day 1 — Setup: name the audience pain
- Format: carousel (8 slides)
- Angle: Mistake + Founder Perspective
- Hook: "I installed 14 AI tools last quarter. My deep work collapsed."
- Body summary: Mira's confession of over-installing AI tools. Sets up the post-chat thesis without naming the launch.
- CTA: save
- Platform: LinkedIn primary

### Day 2 — Setup: name the audience explicitly
- Format: reel (30s)
- Angle: Audience callout + Mechanism
- Hook: "Senior operators don't have more focus. They have fewer surfaces."
- Body summary: 30s talking-head from Mira; explicit audience naming.
- CTA: follow
- Platform: LinkedIn + IG

### Day 3 — Setup: surface the default
- Format: carousel (7 slides)
- Angle: Myth vs Reality
- Hook: "The 5 AI productivity myths killing your week."
- Body summary: Myths the audience holds about AI productivity tools.
- CTA: comment
- Platform: LinkedIn

### Day 4 — Pressure: visible cost
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Visible cost
- Hook: "What 14 AI tools actually cost your week."
- Body summary: Time / decision / context-switching cost made visible.
- CTA: save
- Platform: LinkedIn

### Day 5 — Pressure: founder essay
- Format: long-form post (text + 1 image)
- Angle: Founder Perspective
- Hook: Mira's voice essay on the structural-not-behavioral thesis.
- CTA: follow
- Platform: LinkedIn

### Day 6 — Pressure: contrarian cost
- Format: reel
- Angle: Contrarian
- Hook: "Stop installing AI tools that interrupt you."
- CTA: save
- Platform: TikTok + IG

### Day 7 — Reframe: introduce the mechanism (without naming the product)
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Reframe + Mechanism
- Hook: "The replacement: one read at 8am. One read at 5pm."
- Body summary: Argues for the morning-brief / end-of-day-reset pattern. Doesn't name Lumen.
- CTA: comment
- Platform: LinkedIn

### Day 8 — Reframe: workflow reveal
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Workflow Reveal
- Hook: "How operators run their week without AI chat tools."
- CTA: save
- Platform: LinkedIn + IG

### Day 9 — Reframe: technical breakdown
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Technical Breakdown
- Hook: "Why the chat-shaped UI is the wrong shape for focus."
- CTA: save / DM
- Platform: LinkedIn

### Day 10 — Proof: case anonymized
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Before/After
- Hook: "VP of Engineering at a Series-C devtools company: how the brief changed her week."
- Body summary: Anonymized case from `proof-assets.md`.
- CTA: DM
- Platform: LinkedIn

### Day 11 — Proof: data drop
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Benchmark
- Hook: "What 1,200+ operators say works (and doesn't) in their morning."
- Body summary: NPS, self-reported metrics with sample size.
- CTA: click (full data)
- Platform: LinkedIn

### Day 12 — Proof: founder receipt
- Format: reel
- Angle: Build in Public
- Hook: Theo's engineering essay on the no-chat decision.
- CTA: comment
- Platform: LinkedIn + X

### Day 13 — Ask: launch
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Launch + Product-Led
- Hook: "Today we open Lumen v3.4 Focus Mode."
- Body summary: What it is, who it's for, who it's not, what it costs, what's the trade-off.
- CTA: try (link in bio)
- Platform: LinkedIn + IG + X

### Day 14 — Ask: objection-handling
- Format: carousel
- Angle: Objection Handling
- Hook: "Three things people ask before trying Lumen."
- Body summary: Top 3 objections from the launch-day comments answered honestly.
- CTA: try
- Platform: LinkedIn + IG

## Compression

For a 7-day launch, keep this arc but compress:

- Days 1-2: Setup.
- Days 3-4: Pressure + Reframe.
- Days 5-6: Proof.
- Day 7: Ask.

For a 30-day launch (rare), expand:

- Weeks 1-2: Setup + Pressure (8-12 posts).
- Week 3: Reframe + Proof (6-8 posts).
- Week 4: Ask + Objection handling (4-6 posts).

---

## Required asset inventory

Before generating the campaign, the brief should confirm:

- Hard ship date.
- Public claims allowed (per `constraints.md`).
- Real proof available (per `proof-assets.md`).
- Founder availability for posts that require their voice.
- Image / asset library for each post format.
- Platform plan.

Without these, surface as `[MISSING_INPUT]` and recommend filling before launch.
