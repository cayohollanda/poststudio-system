> Pick the campaign type. Each has a distinctive arc and outcome. Defaults reference [`system/campaign-framework.md`](../../system/campaign-framework.md).

|---|---|---|---|---|

## When to combine types

A 30-day window often runs 2-3 campaigns sequentially (e.g. authority push → proof drop → launch). Each campaign keeps its own arc; they share the audience.

A single campaign should not try to be 3 types at once. Sequence them.
