# Reporting Template

> The end-of-month retrospective. Sent to the client and used internally to refresh the Brand Pack.

---

## Structure

```markdown
# Monthly Report — [Brand] — [Month YYYY]

## Summary (1 paragraph)

[The one paragraph the client reads if they read nothing else. Name the cycle's biggest win and biggest risk.]

## What shipped

| Date | Format | Topic | Pillar | Channel | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD | carousel | [topic] | P1 | LinkedIn | [N saves, M comments] |
| ... | | | | | |

[Total: X posts shipped. Y planned but not shipped (with reason).]

## What worked

1. **[Post type / topic]** — what made it land. Specific.
2. **[...]** — same.
3. **[...]** — same.

## What didn't

1. **[Post type / topic]** — what we'd change. Specific.
2. **[...]** — same.

## Audience signal

- New audience reactions / comments worth quoting (verbatim).
- New vocabulary the audience is using.
- Topics they're asking us about that we haven't covered yet.

## Brand Pack updates we're proposing

- [ ] Add to `voice.md`: [audience-adopted phrase].
- [ ] Add to `proof-assets.md`: [new customer story / quote].
- [ ] Add to `examples.md`: [new anchor post].
- [ ] Add to `rejected-examples.md`: [pattern we banned this month].
- [ ] Refresh `constraints.md`: [new claim / topic that surfaced].

## Risks for next month

- [list]

## Recommended direction for next month

- Theme: [...]
- Active campaigns: [...]
- Cadence: [same / shift].
- Proof to capture before next cycle: [list].

## Action items

| Owner | Action | By when |
|---|---|---|
| Client | Approve Brand Pack updates | YYYY-MM-DD |
| Client | Capture customer story for next proof drop | YYYY-MM-DD |
| Us | Generate next-month plan | YYYY-MM-DD |
```

---

## Cadence

- Run reporting on the last working day of the month.
- Send within 2-3 working days of month-end.
- Pair with the next-month plan delivery for efficiency.

---

## Tone

- Honest. Don't soft-pedal failures.
- Specific. "Engagement went up 12% on Tuesdays" beats "engagement was strong."
- Forward-looking. End with action items, not just retrospective notes.

---

## Without analytics access

If the client does not share analytics:

- Report on what we shipped (counts, formats, topics).
- Report on what the client told us about reactions.
- Surface that performance reporting is best served by analytics access (and recommend a quarterly review).
- Don't fabricate metrics.
