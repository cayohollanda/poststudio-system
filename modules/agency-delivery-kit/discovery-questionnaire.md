# Discovery Questionnaire

> The async form sent before the kickoff call. Aim for ~30 minutes of client time. The depth of these answers determines the depth of the Brand Pack.

---

## Section 1 — Brand basics

1. Brand name + how it's pronounced.
2. One-line plain-language description (no marketing speak).
3. Tagline, if any.
4. Category — what would a stranger search for it under?
5. Stage — pre-launch / early-traction / scaling / established?
6. Website URL.
7. Founders / public-facing voice owners (names, roles, social handles).

## Section 2 — Audience

8. Primary audience — role + situation in 1-2 sentences.
9. Their #1 pain in your category, in their words.
10. What they want they can't currently get.
11. Who is NOT the audience.
12. Where they hang out online (specific places).

## Section 3 — Offer

13. What you sell, in plain language.
14. The unique mechanism — *why* does it work?
15. What it explicitly does NOT do.
16. Pricing tier(s) (public).
17. Top 3 differentiators.
18. Top 3 alternatives the audience considers (including "do nothing").

## Section 4 — Voice

19. 5-7 adjectives describing the voice.
20. 2-3 reference brands whose voice you admire.
21. Words / phrases you love.
22. Words / phrases you ban.
23. Formal or casual? Lowercase-friendly?
24. First-person ("I"), plural ("we"), or third-person?
25. Emoji policy.

## Section 5 — Visual

26. Primary brand colors (hex).
27. Accent color.
28. Typography (headline + body), if known.
29. Visual mood references (films, magazines, brands you admire visually).
30. Visual constraints (no faces, no real screenshots, etc.).

## Section 6 — Proof

31. Best customer story you can share publicly (with permission level).
32. Verbatim quotes / testimonials.
33. Hard numbers (retention, users, revenue) — citable publicly.
34. Awards, press, public artifacts.

## Section 7 — Constraints

35. Compliance posture (HIPAA / GDPR / SEC / FDA / SOC 2 / none).
36. Trigger phrases requiring legal review.
37. Competitors off-limits to name.
38. Topics off-limits.

## Section 8 — Past content

39. 1-3 pieces of YOUR existing content that worked. Why?
40. 1-2 that didn't land. Why?
41. Recurring rituals (e.g. "every Friday we share a build update").

## Section 9 — Goals + cadence

42. Top 3 business goals for the next 90 days.
43. Posting cadence target (per channel).
44. Primary channel.
45. Secondary channels.

## Section 10 — Logistics

46. Who reviews and approves content?
47. What's the review SLA?
48. Where does final content live (CMS, scheduler)?
49. Any existing templates / brand guidelines? Share them.
50. Anything else we should know?

---

## How to send

- Form / shared doc (preferred — async, edits welcome).
- Email with the questions inline (works for resistant clients).
- 30-min discovery call recorded with permission (last resort).

---

## Honest answers > complete answers

Tell the client: it's better to leave fields blank than to invent answers. The Brand Pack process will surface the gaps and fill them through the kickoff call and follow-ups.
