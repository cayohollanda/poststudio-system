# Revision Rules

> The default revision policy. Adapt the numbers to your engagement model.

---

## Default policy

- **1 revision round included** per deliverable.
- Round = a single back-and-forth with the client. Multiple notes in one feedback round count as one round.
- Additional revision rounds → billed per the engagement's revision rate.

---

## What counts as a revision

- Tweaks to copy (replacement words, tightened sentences, clarified slides).
- Tone / voice adjustments.
- Removing a slide or shortening the carousel.
- Changing the CTA.
- Updating image-prompt direction.
- Replacing one slide's headline.

---

## What does NOT count as a revision (it's a new deliverable)

- Switching the angle entirely (mistake → contrarian).
- Switching the visual mode mid-stream.
- Re-targeting a different audience.
- Adding 2+ new slides.
- Switching format (carousel → reel).
- Changing the brand voice for one post.

These trigger a new generation, not a revision.

---

## What happens when feedback contradicts the Brand Pack

Don't silently comply. Surface the conflict:

> "The requested change conflicts with [Brand Pack section]. Specifically: [reason]. We have three options: [A — apply but flag for next refresh], [B — refresh the Brand Pack first], [C — keep the original]. Which do you prefer?"

The client decides; we document.

---

## What happens when feedback contradicts the safety / claims rules

Never comply with a request to invent numbers, customers, awards, or quotes. Even if the client insists.

> "I can't add that specific number without a source from `proof-assets.md`. We can either: [A — get the real number from you], [B — use a placeholder like 'noticeably faster'], [C — remove the claim]. Which?"

This is a hard line. Hold it.

---

## SLA on revisions

- Revision turnaround: 24-48h after feedback received.
- If the deliverable is time-sensitive, surface it in the handoff — don't assume.

---

## Revision capture

Use `client-feedback-form.md` to capture each revision request. Maintain a per-engagement log:

```
brands/[client-slug]/feedback-log.md
```

Patterns in the log inform Brand Pack refresh.

---

## When the client requests > 2 revision rounds

- Round 3 → recommend a "structural conversation" to align before continuing tweaks.
- Round 4 → likely a brief misalignment; pause and reset.
- Round 5+ → engagement has lost focus; renegotiate or step back.

A deliverable that needs 5 revisions usually has a Brand Pack problem upstream, not a writing problem.
