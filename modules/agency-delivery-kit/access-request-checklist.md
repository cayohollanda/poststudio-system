# Access Request Checklist

> What you need from the client to start. Sent alongside the discovery questionnaire.

---

## Required (hard blockers)

- [ ] Brand assets: logo files, color palette (hex), typography files (or names).
- [ ] Recent content samples: 5-10 posts they've shipped, in their original format.
- [ ] Founder voice samples: 1-2 essays / videos / talks the founder has done publicly.
- [ ] Customer evidence: at least 1 quote, story, or metric they're willing to use publicly (with permission level).
- [ ] Calendar / posting context: who posts, where, with what cadence today.
- [ ] Approval flow: who approves content before publishing, with what SLA.

If any of these are missing, the engagement starts in degraded mode. Surface it on Day 0.

---

## Strongly recommended

- [ ] Brand guidelines doc (if any).
- [ ] Internal style guide (if any).
- [ ] Customer interview transcripts.
- [ ] Sales call recordings (with permission).
- [ ] Internal positioning doc.
- [ ] Existing content calendar.
- [ ] Analytics / performance data on past content.

---

## Optional but useful

- [ ] Competitor reference set (3-5 — we'll never reproduce, just calibrate).
- [ ] Mood board (films, magazines, brands they admire visually).
- [ ] Public roadmap.
- [ ] Press / award list.

---

## Access requests (NOT credentials)

We never ask for:

- Login credentials to social accounts. Client publishes; we deliver.
- Access to email / Slack / internal data tools.
- Customer contact info (we communicate via the client).

When the engagement requires something that *looks* like credential access, document the alternative path.

---

## Format for delivery

The client sends:

- A shared folder (Drive, Notion, Dropbox) named `[brand-slug]-content-engagement`.
- Each item lives in a clearly named sub-folder.
- The client owns the folder; we have view/comment access.

---

## Storage policy

- We do not retain client materials after the engagement ends except those baked into the Brand Pack (with permission).
- Client materials are not used to train future generations of any model unless explicitly permitted.
- We do not share client materials across other engagements.

---

## When to start anyway

If the client misses 2-3 items but provides enough to define audience + voice + offer + at least one piece of proof, we can start with a degraded foundation. Mark gaps as `[MISSING_INPUT]` in the Brand Pack and revisit weekly.

If the client misses everything — wait. The engagement starts when the foundation is sufficient.
