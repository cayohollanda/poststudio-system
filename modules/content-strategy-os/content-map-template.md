# Content Map Template

> The 30-day plan. One row per planned post. Each post tied to a pillar.

---

## Format

```markdown
## 30-day content plan — [Brand]

**Period:** [YYYY-MM-DD to YYYY-MM-DD]
**Cadence:** [N posts per week × M channels]
**Primary channel:** [linkedin / instagram / etc.]

### Week 1

| Day | Topic | Format | Pillar | Channel | Primary CTA | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Mon | [topic] | carousel | Pillar 1 | LinkedIn | save | series anchor |
| Wed | [topic] | reel | Pillar 2 | IG + LinkedIn | DM | proof drop |
| Fri | [topic] | carousel | Pillar 1 | LinkedIn | follow | founder essay |

### Week 2 — 4

[same format]

---

## Cadence guidance

| Brand stage | Recommended cadence |
|---|---|
| early-traction | 3-4 posts/week, 1 primary channel |
| scaling | 4-5 posts/week, 1-2 primary channels |
| established | 4-6 posts/week, 2-3 channels with adaptation |

Don't recommend a cadence the brand has stated it can't maintain. Posting cliffs hurt more than slow consistency.

---

## Channel adaptation table

| Channel | Format priority | Caption length | Cadence sweet spot |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | carousel, founder essay, single image | 150-250 words | 3-5/week |
| Instagram | carousel, reels | 100-180 words | 4-6/week |
| TikTok | reels, carousels | 80-140 words | 4-7/week |
| X | thread, single image | 100-280 chars per tweet | 5-10/week |
| Threads | shorter caption, image | 100-200 words | 3-5/week |

When a post is multi-channel, name the primary and adaptation notes per channel.

---

## Anchor placement rules

- **Series posts** → fixed day of week (e.g. Operator Essay every Wednesday).
- **Launch days** → Mondays or Tuesdays for B2B audiences.
- **Proof drops** → mid-week, ideally same day as a series for compounding traffic.
- **Founder essays** → weekly cadence, never daily.
- **Light/educational posts** → fill the gaps between heavier posts.

---

## What goes in `Notes` column

- "Series anchor" — this is the recurring series post.
- "Proof drop" — this post lands a metric or case from `proof-assets.md`.
- "Launch day" — primary CTA is `try`.
- "Cross-post" — this post adapts to a second channel.
- "Founder voice required" — this post comes from Mira / Theo / [founder name].
- "Image asset needed" — this post requires a visual asset not yet in the asset library.
- `[PROOF_NEEDED]` — this post depends on proof not yet captured.

---

## Output discipline

- One table per week.
- Topics are *categories*, not full titles.
- Every post tied to one pillar (no multi-pillar entries; if a topic spans two, pick the primary).
- Channel and primary CTA per post.
- Notes column carries the production-relevant flags.

---

## What this map is not

- Not a list of full carousel briefs. Each row triggers a future call to `prompts/04-generate-carousel.md` (or equivalent).
- Not a project tracker. Status, deadlines, and approvals live in the CRUD platform.
- Not immutable. Refresh weekly; replan monthly.
