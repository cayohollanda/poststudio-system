# Quality Checklist — Content Strategy OS

> Strategy-specific checks. Run before returning to the user.

---

## Critical (any fail → don't ship)

- [ ] 3-5 pillars defined.
- [ ] Each pillar has an argument, audience pain, belief-being-challenged, topic clusters (8-15), dominant content type, funnel position.
- [ ] 2-4 recurring series defined, each with cadence, format, anchor structure, pillar tag.
- [ ] 30-day plan present (week-by-week or day-by-day).
- [ ] Every plan entry tagged with topic, format, pillar, channel, primary CTA.
- [ ] Content-to-offer map present.
- [ ] Distribution plan present.
- [ ] Missing inputs surfaced.

## Quality (each fail → flag)

- [ ] No two pillars overlap excessively.
- [ ] No invented stats or audience-size claims.
- [ ] Cadence respects the brand's stated capacity (no 7/week if the team is one person).
- [ ] At least one recurring series matches each major pillar.
- [ ] Plan includes both TOFU and MOFU/BOFU posts.
- [ ] Plan includes proof drops (when proof is available).
- [ ] Plan accounts for upcoming launches / events from the brief.
- [ ] Plan includes founder-voice posts at the right cadence (per Brand Pack).
- [ ] No banned topics from `constraints.md` appear in the plan.
- [ ] 90-day direction sketches a coherent arc, not just "more of the same."

---

## Anti-patterns

- **Strategy without pillars.** A 30-day plan that's just topics with no organizing argument.
- **Strategy without distribution.** Pillars defined, plan written, no thought given to how each post lives across channels.
- **Strategy that doesn't tie to the offer.** Posts that wouldn't change anyone's mind about buying.
- **Cadence cliff.** 5 posts in week 1, 1 post in week 2. Smooth out.
- **Series stack without owners.** 4 series proposed, no one assigned to maintain.

---

## Approval

Strategy passes when all critical pass and ≥7 of 10 quality items pass. Otherwise return with explicit gaps and recommend a follow-up planning session.
