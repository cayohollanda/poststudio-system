# Positioning — `lumen`

> Lumen is the focus operating system for senior operators in scaling SaaS who want their morning back without installing another chat-shaped AI tool.

---

## Positioning statement

**Lumen is the focus operating system for senior operators at 50-500 person SaaS who want their morning back without installing another chat-shaped AI tool.**

---

## Why this positioning

The "AI productivity" category is saturated with chat-shaped tools. Senior operators are quietly experiencing the opposite of what those tools promise: more interruption, less deep work. We position against the *interaction model*, not against any specific competitor. The category we own is "post-chat AI" — a category we're partly inventing.

---

## Category framing

- **Category we claim:** focus operating system / post-chat productivity tool.
- **Category we are NOT in:** AI assistant, chat tool, calendar app, second-brain app, ADHD intervention.
- **What category-level argument we make:** the senior-operator advantage is structural, not behavioral. Tools should remove decision surfaces, not add them.

---

## Differentiation matrix

| Dimension | Lumen | Calendar AI (e.g. Reclaim, Clockwise category) | General AI Chat | Second-Brain Apps | "Do nothing" |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Interaction model | no chat; one read at 8am, one at 5pm | calendar-shaped | chat-shaped | capture-shaped | n/a |
| Decision load | reduces (auto-defends blocks) | shifts (re-arranges) | adds (prompts to write) | adds (capture creates open loops) | none change |
| Deep-work hours/week | aims for 2 protected blocks/day | helps schedule them | actively interrupts | unchanged | unchanged |
| Onboarding time | <4 minutes | 10-30 minutes | <2 minutes | 30+ minutes (system setup) | 0 minutes |
| What we don't do | answer Q&A, run meetings, transcribe | calendar visualization | one-off exploration | knowledge management | nothing |

---

## What we promise

1. **Your morning is yours by 8:30am.** The brief is read once. The day's plan survives.
2. **Two protected deep-work blocks per day.** Defended without you negotiating.
3. **One read at end-of-day, then close the laptop.** No "review your day" guilt loop.
4. **No chat. Ever.** This is structural, not aesthetic.
5. **No training on your data.** What we process for you stays scoped to your account.

---

## What we will not become

We will never be a chat tool. We will not add a sidebar assistant. We will not become a calendar app or another inbox. The minute Lumen has a "How can I help you today?" prompt, we've failed our own thesis.
