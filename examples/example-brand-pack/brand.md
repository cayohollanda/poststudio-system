## Elevator pitch

Most "AI productivity" tools today are chat windows pretending to be assistants. They interrupt your day, demand attention, and scatter your work across 14 tabs. Lumen flips that. It runs in the background, watches your calendar and inbox, and produces a single morning brief: what to focus on, what to defer, what to kill. It's not a chat. It's an operating posture.

## Why we exist

Senior operators in 50-500 person SaaS companies are getting *less* done with AI than without it — because the modal interface for AI is "open a chat and ask." That format is the opposite of deep work. Lumen exists to give operators the AI leverage without the AI interruptions.

## What we will not become

We will never be a chat tool. We will not add a sidebar assistant. We will not become a calendar app or another inbox. The minute Lumen has a "How can I help you today?" prompt, we've failed our own thesis.

## How to pronounce / spell

"Lumen" — pronounced *LOO-men*. Always lowercase in body copy. Capitalized only at the start of a sentence, in headlines, or as a brand mark.
