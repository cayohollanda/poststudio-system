# Example Reels Script — `lumen` (fictional)

> Worked example. 30-second faceless TikTok / Reels script.

## Strategic Angle

Faceless 30s reel adapting the carousel's confessional angle ("I installed 14 AI tools last quarter") for short-form. Sound-off legible; b-roll-led; founder voiceover (Mira) on top.

## Script

- Type: faceless (no on-camera person; b-roll cuts + voiceover)
- Duration: 30s
- Word count target: ~70

### Beat 1 — Hook (0:00-0:02)
- Voice / on-screen: "Most AI tools quietly destroy your deep work."
- Visual / B-roll: Cinematic close-up of a fractured glass dome over a wooden desk in dusk light.
- Audio note: cold open; no music.

### Beat 2 — Set-up (0:02-0:05)
- Voice / on-screen: "And the chat is why."
- Visual / B-roll: Slow zoom on a paper calendar page being torn — a meeting killed.
- Audio note: subtle ambient enters at 0:03.

### Beat 3 — Body (0:05-0:18)
- Voice (Mira): "I installed fourteen AI productivity tools last quarter. By the end, my deep work had been cut in half. The tools weren't the problem. The interaction model was. Every chat box is an interruption with a friendly face."
- Visual / B-roll: Quick cuts (3-4) of small glass jars on a shelf multiplying into haze; hands closing a laptop deliberately; a single brass key on a desk.
- Audio note: ambient swells through this beat.

### Beat 4 — Payoff (0:18-0:24)
- Voice (Mira): "Senior operators don't have more focus. They have fewer surfaces."
- Visual / B-roll: Single sealed cream envelope on a dark wooden desk, lit by a warm rim light.
- Audio note: ambient resolves; near-silence at 0:23.

### Beat 5 — CTA (0:24-0:30)
- Voice / on-screen: "Save this for the next time you're about to install another AI tool."
- Visual / B-roll: Close-up of a brass key on the desk; the wordmark "lumen" appears at the bottom-right.
- Audio note: ambient out; brand sting (subtle, 0.5s).
